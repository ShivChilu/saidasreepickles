Our Story

Traditional Recipes, Modern Packaging

Saidasree Pickles was founded in 2010 with a mission to bring authentic homemade pickles to every household. Our recipes have been passed down through generations, preserving the traditional flavors while incorporating modern food safety standards.

Each batch is carefully prepared in small quantities to ensure quality and freshness. We use only the finest ingredients sourced from local farmers whenever possible.
